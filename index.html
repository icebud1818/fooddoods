Each Food Dood has its own unique rarity. From the most common to the rarest, every character brings a different flavor and value to the collection.

Our Roadmap

Phase 1: Launch the Food Doods NFT Collection

The official Food Doods mint will take place on OpenSea on a date to be announced. All 76 unique characters will be available for purchase, with pricing details coming soon. Get ready to join the Food Doods community!

Phase 2: Release of the Kitchen Pass

All Food Doods NFT holders at the time of the wallet snapshot will receive an airdrop of 3 Kitchen Passes! These passes unlock the ability to combine your Food Doods into unique recipes, with new recipes released weekly. Completing certain recipes will reward you with special prizes. The only items consumed when creating recipes are your Kitchen Passes. Additional airdrops of Kitchen Passes will occur at regular intervals throughout the year. Your Food Doods' backgrounds may transfer to the recipes, and legendary Food Doods will have exclusive recipes of their own!

Phase 3: Expand into the Metaverse

Food Doods will journey into the metaverse, bringing their unique charm to a range of exciting merchandise and immersive Web3 games! Get ready for a fully interactive experience where the world of Food Doods extends beyond NFTs and into the digital universe.
